What We Do

Providing transportation to seniors and people with visual impairments

We operate within Gainesville, Georgia

``

CHARACTERISTICS OF THE SERVICE

Available 7 days a week, 24 hours a day for any purpose
Available for health and wellness trips 5 miles outside of the perimeter of our service area
Rides are provided in private automobiles by trained drivers
Membership based - persons 60 years and older and the visually impaired 21 years and older, are eligible to join
Rides may be booked at any time; discounts are applied for shared rides and advance notice
Riders may travel alone or with others, with 20% discounts for shared rides
Community based affiliates are supported by private, rather than public resources
Affordable fares that are half the price of a comparable taxi ride
Not "just a taxi" - drivers provide arm-through-arm, door-to-door service and help with packages
No money is exchanged in the vehicle, and tips are not accepted
Riders pre-fund a personal transportation account, and a monthly statement details all payments and charges
Uses automobiles, rather than vans or buses
